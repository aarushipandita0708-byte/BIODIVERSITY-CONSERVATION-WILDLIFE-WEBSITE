About Biodiversity

Biodiversity is the variety of living organisms found on Earth. It includes plants, animals, birds, insects, and microorganisms. Biodiversity helps maintain the balance of nature and supports all forms of life.

Importance of Biodiversity

  • Provides food and medicine.
  • Maintains ecological balance.
  • Supports agriculture and forests.
  • Keeps the environment healthy.

Threats to Biodiversity

  • Deforestation
  • Pollution
  • Climate change
  • Illegal hunting of wildlife

Conservation of Biodiversity

  1. Plant more trees.
  2. Protect forests and wildlife.
  3. Reduce pollution.
  4. Use natural resources wisely.
  5. Create awareness among people.

Take the biodiversity pledge

Conclusion

Biodiversity is essential for all living beings. Everyone should help protect nature so that future generations can enjoy a clean and healthy environment.
